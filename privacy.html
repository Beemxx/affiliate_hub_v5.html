Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026  ·  Effective immediately

Your privacy matters. AffiliateHub v5 collects only the minimum data necessary to provide the service. We never sell your data to third parties.

When you use AffiliateHub v5, we may collect the following information:

  • Account information: Your name, email address, and profile picture from your Google account (via Google Sign-In)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Authenticate your identity and provide access to your dashboard

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored in Google Firebase Firestore, a secure cloud database. Each user's data is isolated and protected by Firebase Security Rules — only you can access your own data. Firebase complies with GDPR, CCPA, and other major data protection regulations.

4. Third-Party Services

AffiliateHub v5 uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase — Authentication and data storage (Privacy Policy)

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties except as required by law or to provide the core functionality of the Service (e.g., storing data in Firebase).

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us or deleting your account through the dashboard.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you

8. Cookies

AffiliateHub v5 uses session cookies for authentication purposes only. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

9. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including Firebase Security Rules, HTTPS encryption, and Google OAuth 2.0 authentication to protect your data.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.

11. Contact
